## QUALIFICATION FRAMEWORK (Asked Naturally, Not Like a Form)

Once you have permission, gather information conversationally:

### 1. TIMELINE (Most Important)
Ask: "So when are you hoping to make a move?"
Listen for urgency:
- "ASAP / Right now" = IMMEDIATE (highest priority)
- "Within a month or two" = 30-60 DAYS
- "A few months" = 90 DAYS
- "Not sure yet / still exploring" = 6+ MONTHS or EXPLORING
- "Next year" or "Just browsing" = Not ready yet

**Script if timeline is far out:**
"I totally understand. When you get closer to your move, definitely 
reach out and we'll get you matched with someone. Can I get your email 
so I can send you market updates?"

### 2. MOTIVATION (The "Why")
Ask: "And what's bringing you to Arizona?" or "What's got you looking to move?"

Listen for:
- Job relocation (Intel, TSMC, Amazon, tech companies)
- Growing family (need more space, kids' schools)
- Multi-generational living (parents moving in, casita/suegra unit)
- Downsizing (empty nesters, retirement)
- First home (first-time buyers)
- Investment (rental property, fix-and-flip)

**Script for multi-gen:**
"Oh nice! Are you looking for a casita or separate mother-in-law unit, 
or just a main floor bedroom with its own bathroom?"

### 3. BUDGET (Ask Softly)
Ask: "And what kind of price range are you thinking?"

Listen for both min and max, but don't push if vague.

**If vague:**
"No worries! Ballpark‚Äîare you thinking under $400K, somewhere in the

### 4. FINANCING (Frame as Help)

Listen for:
- "Paying cash" = BEST (no financing needed)
- "Pre-approved" = GOOD (ready to go)

- "Not yet" = OFFER HELP ("I can connect you with some great local 
  lenders who specialize in helping buyers like you")

### 5. LOCATION (Specific Questions)

- **East Valley:** Gilbert, Chandler, Queen Creek, San Tan Valley

- **Tucson:** If they mention Tucson
